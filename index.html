About VEMC

VEMC Vocabulary Trainer

A fast, minimalistic flashcard tool designed for mastering vocabulary. It utilizes the Free Dictionary API and Google Translate to provide English meanings, Bengali translations, and contextual examples automatically.

Created by Sakib Nawar Tanmoy.
